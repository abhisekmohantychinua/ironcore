About Iron Core

Iron Core exists to help individuals push beyond their limits. Strength isn’t just physical — it’s mental, emotional, and disciplined. Every session, every rep, every challenge is crafted to help you grow stronger, more confident, and unstoppable.

Our Philosophy

We embrace the mindset of "Hustle for Health." Discipline, consistency, and resilience are at the core of everything we do.

State-of-the-Art Equipment

Premium machinery and free weights designed to help you train like a professional athlete.

Expert Coaches

Certified trainers guide you safely and effectively, every step of the way.

Community & Support

Join a community that motivates, encourages, and celebrates your progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your gym timings?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Sundays are for rest and maintenance.
Do you offer personal training?
Yes, we have certified personal trainers available. Book via our contact form or at the reception.
What memberships do you offer?
Monthly, quarterly, and yearly memberships. Students and corporate discounts available.
Is there a trial session?
Yes! We offer a free trial session for first-time visitors. Book via our contact form or call us directly.
Do you have nutrition guidance?
Absolutely! Our trainers provide basic nutrition tips and customized diet plans on request.
